Who is the competition?

the main competition would be online retail stores like shopify and square space. these providers offer prebuilt websites that only work.

What do I do about this?

my goal is to keep the simplicity and easy use of the site while making it look nice and have smooth animation.

What is the competition doing right?

In my opinion, shopify does a super good job at keeping things simple. for example, everything in the page is split into blocks and has a super simple navigation feature

What is the competition doing wrong?

I feel that the flaw with shopify and square space is that everyone uses the same page. You can only have so many copies of "dusk" before it becomes repetitive.
